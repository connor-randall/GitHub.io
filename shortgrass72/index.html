Crafting Your Perfect Golf Experience

Short Grass 72 is built around one simple idea: the best shots happen on the short grass. In golf, 72 is par, the standard, the benchmark, the score everyone chases. The short grass is where confidence begins, where control shows up, and where the game feels right. We design gear for golfers who respect the game but don’t overthink it. Clean design. Premium materials. No noise. Just golf gear that you'll actually want to wear, whether you're on the course, at the range, or anywhere in between. Short Grass 72 isn't about chasing perfection; it’s about playing smart, staying composed, and keeping the ball on the short grass.
